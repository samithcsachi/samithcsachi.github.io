About Me

I am a Machine Learning Engineer in the making, with a strong foundation in data analysis, business intelligence, and logistics coordination. With 4 years in BI and 13 years in logistics, I bring a unique blend of industry knowledge and technical expertise, enabling me to drive data-driven solutions and innovation.

My journey started in logistics, where I developed a keen eye for process optimization and operational efficiency. Over time, my passion for data analytics and automation led me to transition into data science and machine learning, where I now focus on building predictive models, developing intelligent dashboards, and solving real-world problems using AI-driven insights.

I actively participate in Kaggle competitions, honing my ML skills and staying up-to-date with the latest advancements in data science. Additionally, I leverage Power BI and Tableau to create compelling visual narratives that drive business decisions.

I believe in continuous learning, always striving to improve processes, expand my knowledge, and mentor others in the field.

About Me

I’m originally from Kannur, Kerala, India—a coastal city affectionately known as the “Land of Theyyams” for its vibrant ritual art form that’s woven into the fabric of local culture. I graduated with a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science, Electronics, and Mathematics from Kristu Jayanti College, Bangalore University, Bengaluru.

Although I studied computer science, my career began in logistics. Like many from Kerala, I moved to the UAE in search of opportunities, and destiny led me to the world of logistics. Over time, my work shifted toward data analytics, and I found myself drawn to data science. Certifications from Google and IBM, along with tackling hands-on projects, confirmed for me that machine learning was the path I wanted to follow. Listening to Kaggle podcasts and seeing how machine learning is used in the real world sparked my motivation to dive deeper into this field.

During my last four years in logistics, data analytics became an integral part of my role, giving me a practical perspective on how real-world operations can be transformed by data-driven decisions. While I’m especially excited about computer vision projects and look forward to exploring them, so far I’ve earned a 3 x Kaggle Expert designation—an achievement I’m quite proud of.

I want to harness machine learning to solve problems that matter, striving to develop solutions with real-world impact. Beyond my professional life, I'm a passionate cricket fan, love to travel, and enjoy discovering new foods whenever possible. At my core, I believe in continuous learning and bring a “love and be human” approach to everything I do. If you share the mindset of learning and growing together, I’d love to connect!

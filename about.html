About Me

Data Analyst with a solid foundation in computer science and extensive experience in logistics coordination. Despite a career gap where I dedicated 13 years to honing my skills in the logistics field, I've returned to my roots in technology, eager to apply my analytical mindset and technical knowledge to the realm of data analysis. I began my career as a Logistics Professional and through a process of work experience combined with exploration and education, guided my career into an area I love - data analytics

I'm passionate about using data to drive improvements. I analyze everything - particularly data!

As a Data Professional, I have a strong passion for developing Dashboards, Reports and Data Models.

I have a track record of solving analytical problems. My analytical mind-set and attention to detail allowed me to lead complex projects and initiatives to drive operational and process improvements.

I am a life-long learner and strive daily to improve not only processes, but myself. I believe learning is an ever-ending process and we need to keep on learning new skills and need to become linguists in the field.

AREAS I EXCEL: Data Analysis, Data Collection and Organization, Data Visualization, Project Leadership and Coordination, Reporting, Process Improvement, Quality Assurance, Cross-Functional Collaboration, Business Intelligence, Problem-Solving, Client Management, Training
